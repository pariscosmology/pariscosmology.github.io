Paris Cosmology Meetings

About PaCo

First established in 2022, the goal of the PaCo meetings is to regularly bring together cosmologists in the Paris region for talks, discussions and the stimulation of new collaborations.

The meetings take place for one afternoon every 3-4 months, hosted in turn by one of the participating institutes.

Each meeting consists of an opening talk by an invited speaker, followed by a coffee break, short contributed talks and a social gathering.

"Paco" is the diminutive form of the name "Francisco" in Spanish, meaning "Frenchman".
